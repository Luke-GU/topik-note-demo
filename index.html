Why TOPIK matters

TOPIK is the official Korean proficiency exam — required for student visas, work permits, and university admission in Korea. 490,000 people took it in 2024 (an all-time high), and the number has doubled in just 4 years. If you're serious about studying or working in Korea, your TOPIK score is your ticket in.

Who this is for

Foreign adult learners who must pass TOPIK level 3 or higher within 3–6 months for a student visa, work permit, or graduate admission — and who can only study 30–60 minutes a day during their commute.
